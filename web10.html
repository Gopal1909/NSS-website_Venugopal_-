Who are we?

National Service Scheme IIT Madras is a student run organisation that was initiated over four decades ago with a vision, to cause benefit to the society is any possible way. We at NSS function with a mission to mobilise our the youth into a sect of progressive citizens and imbibe in them an inclination towards social service.

What do we do?

NSS IIT Madras, intake more than 300 active students each year, from all respective years and batches, who help contribute to the cause of the scheme. Over the years we have undergone a series of structural and ideological changes in order to foster creativity and innovation in all our activities. By working out projects from Braille magazines to technology interventions, from teaching children in urban slums to educational video content, NSS, IITM seeks to challenge the mediocre thinking, and reach out into the darkness, to pull a hand into the light.
